How to Flash Image?

  • a. Download image first in your host pc with Ubuntu

    https://drive.google.com/open?id=12XSRVi0IwQ5S7I-T75rbN5Aq4Quk0CjN

  • b. Follow video and flash image

    https://drive.google.com/open?id=1Mu40rDiNP1Jggak3bWoyDDgGCxQXEVyX
    • i. Power on Neon-i1000
    • ii.Enter force recovery mode

      You can refer to pin define below, and try to enter recover mode

      • step1. Hold recovery btn (short pin5+pin6)
      • step2. Press reset btn (short pin3+pint4)

    • iii. Prepare host pc, and flash image through usb cable
