How to flash image?

It needs to go force recovery mode first before flash image. Here’s the video which guild you how to flash image.

Step 1: Download image first from your host pc with Ubuntu

Download link: https://drive.google.com/open?id=12XSRVi0IwQ5S7I-T75rbN5Aq4Quk0CjN

Step 2: Follow video and flash image

Video link: https://drive.google.com/open?id=1Mu40rDiNP1Jggak3bWoyDDgGCxQXEVyX

  • Power on Neon-i1000.

  • Enter force recovery mode.

    You can refer to pin define below, and try to enter recover mode.

    • Step 1: Hold recovery btn (short pin5+pin6)
    • Step 2: Press reset btn (short pin3+pint4)
