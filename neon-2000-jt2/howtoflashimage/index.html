How to flash image?

It needs to go force recovery mode first before flash image. Here’s the video which guild you how to flash image.

Step 2: Follow video and flash image

  • Video link1

  • at NEON-2000-JT2/JNX

    • Power on NEON-2000-JT2/JNX.
    • Enter force recovery mode.

  • at Host PC

    • Unzip the file downloading from Step 1
    $ tar -zxvf Linux_for_Tegra_JetPack43.tar.gz

    or

    $ tar -jxvf A4_Linux_for_Tegra.20200528_JT2_JP43_v1.0.2.tbz2

    You can refer to pin define below, and try to enter recover mode.

    • Step 1: Hold recovery btn (short pin5+pin6)

    • Step 2: Press reset btn (short pin3+pint4)

    • Go to flash folder

    cd Linux_for_Tegra_JetPack43
    • Flash the Neon-2000-JT2
    sudo ./flash.sh -r jetson-tx2 mmcblk0p1
    • Flash the Neon-2000-JNX
    sudo ./flash.sh
